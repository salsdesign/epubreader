# Pocket Library

An EPUB reader that tracks your reading statistics, bookmarks, and annotations.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1.  Upload an EPUB file to start reading.
2.  Your library is saved locally in your browser.
3.  Track your reading time and pages read under stats.

## 📦 Features

- **EPUB Support**: Basic EPUB 2/3 support.
- **Reading Stats**: Track time spent and pages read.
- **Bookmarks & Annotations**: Save your progress and thoughts.
- **Library Management**: Keep multiple books in your browser.
- **Export**: Export your stats to JSON, CSV, or TXT.

## 🛠 Required Environment Variables

- `GEMINI_API_KEY`: Required if AI features are added later (not used in base version).
